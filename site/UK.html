UNITED KINGDOM

I moved to the UK from Italy when I was 13 years old. At first I found the country to be very different from everything I was used to: culture-, food- and people-wise. However, with passing time I grew to appreciate the country more and more. Now, after moving away from the UK, I regard it as my home.
